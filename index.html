Projects

rcpdcp

A functional and clean portfolio website for an artist/content creative. rcpdcp, which can be deciphered as recomposer/decomposer, is a dual web universe of a visual artist/(copy)writer-music journalist. The website, which is soon to be regularly updated with new content, reflects the client’s two main creative outlets.

Recomposer is a visually grounded concept which invites to recreate (recompose) her ambiguous artworks relying on your own imagination. Decomposer represents the client’s analytical part of the brain as it comes to writing about music as well as her background in the promotional side of the music business.

CyberSpaceWhat

For those who care about their web privacy. Nothing lasts forever, unless you upload to the Internet - it’s a realization that has become painfully obvious over the course of the past few years. With each click, view, stream, upload and download you are giving free and easy access to your personal data which is then being collected, shared and sold without you having a say in any of that.

CyberSpaceWhat.com, my first commercial project, is a place to go if you’re looking for credible and researched information about your options when it comes to protecting your data from malicious individuals, businesses or governments with the best tools on the market. Future expansions were planned until the client amended his business plans.
